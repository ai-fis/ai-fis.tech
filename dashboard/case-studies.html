Case Studies

AI-FIS Technologies is built from real-world experience and responds to real-world needs. Below are key deployments and initiatives that demonstrate the platform’s power to expose hidden signals and restore public accountability.

Kenya – Spotlight on Integrity

Born from the need to track and sonify systemic abuse in public finance and procurement fraud, AI-FIS was first conceived as a response to financial irregularities and asset concealment in Kenya. Our dashboard now provides sonic insight into ghost workers, unverified suppliers, and real-time money flow.

By connecting AI pattern recognition with human-audible feedback, we empower investigators, journalists, and civil society with an intuitive way to “hear” fiscal corruption. In real-time, fraud leaves a trace. AI-FIS ensures it leaves a sound.

East Africa Regional Deployment

AI-FIS is being developed as a multi-nodal, post-quantum secure platform for the African continent. With integration points identified in Kenya, Rwanda, and Uganda, we aim to deliver the first harmonized public integrity sound system across national boundaries.

Each node sonifies the local data context: procurement anomalies, budget drift, debt exposure. The shared intelligence becomes a harmonic fingerprint of fiscal trust.
